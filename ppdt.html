About PPDT:
The Picture Perception and Discussion Test (PPDT) is a part of the SSB selection process. Candidates are shown a picture for a short time and must write a story based on it. This test assesses your observation, imagination, and communication skills, as well as your ability to work in a group during the discussion phase.
What is PPDT?

Select a practice set to view the image for 30 seconds and than write a generic story on that picture in 4 minutes
